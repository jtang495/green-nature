Frequently Asked Questions

May I pick my own vegetables?

Yes. We welcome our customers to pick up their own vegetables and fruits. But you must make an appointment (contact)

Can I make reservations?

Yes. We welcome reservations from you. We can prepare the produce ready for pick up at set time so that you can save time.

Is your farm certified?

Yes. We are a registered farm in Maryland and are authorized to produce and sell farm produce and foods to the public.

Who is responsible for accidents at the farm?

Although we take care to make our farm a safe place, please acknowledge that a farm is an inherently dangerous work place where people can get hurt in many ways. We are not responsible for any bodily and property accidents and damages at our farm. Those who are concerned should not enter our farm and can use our off-farm distribution service to purchase our farm produce.
