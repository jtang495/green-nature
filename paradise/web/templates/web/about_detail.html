Our Mission

We believe in holistic farming. Over the years, with the advancement of civilization, our food has completely changed from the food of our ancestors. Our goal is to return to the past, to the traditional agricultural methods around for centuries.

If you’re looking for Grass-Fed & Grass-Finished, Pasture-Raised, Free-Range & Cage-Free, Humanely-Raised, All Natural, non-GMO, Not Fed Antibiotics or Animal By-Products, Organic (non-certified), we’re your farm.

Animals

Animals at Green Acres Farm roam freely within their open and spacious pens. They eat 100% natural feed free of hormones and chemicals. You can taste the difference.

Plants

We use absolutely no pesticides on our plants. Doing so reduces our harvest but any other way of farming is not natural. High quality food comes at a cost. We use crop rotation as a natural method of increasing yield.
